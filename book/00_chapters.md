# English Book Chapters (A1 to C2)

## 1. Fundamentals (A1-A2)
**1.1 Chapter summary:** Basic building blocks of English including the alphabet, pronunciation, basic grammar structures, and essential vocabulary for everyday situations.

**2. Sub sections:**
- The English Alphabet and Pronunciation
- Personal Pronouns and To Be
- Present Simple Tense
- Articles (a, an, the)
- Basic Question Formation
- Common Nouns and Adjectives
- Numbers, Dates, and Time
- Essential Everyday Vocabulary

## 2. Elementary Communication (A2)
**2.1 Chapter summary:** Expanding communication skills with more verb tenses, prepositions, and vocabulary for familiar topics and routine tasks.

**2. Sub sections:**
- Present Continuous Tense
- Past Simple Tense
- Prepositions of Time and Place
- Countable and Uncountable Nouns
- Basic Comparatives and Superlatives
- Modal Verbs (can, could, should)
- Common Phrasal Verbs

## 3. Intermediate Structures (B1)
**3.1 Chapter summary:** Developing more complex grammar and expanding vocabulary to handle most everyday situations and express opinions.

**2. Sub sections:**
- Present Perfect Tense
- Past Continuous Tense
- Future Tenses (will, going to)
- Conditionals (Zero, First, Second)
- Passive Voice (Introduction)
- Relative Clauses
- Reported Speech (Basics)
- Connectors and Linking Words

## 4. Upper Intermediate Mastery (B2)
**4.1 Chapter summary:** Refining language skills with advanced grammar, nuanced expressions, and the ability to discuss abstract topics.

**2. Sub sections:**
- Present Perfect Continuous
- Past Perfect Tenses
- Third Conditional and Mixed Conditionals
- Advanced Modal Verbs (might, ought to, must)
- Passive Voice (All Forms)
- Advanced Phrasal Verbs
- Reported Speech (Advanced)
- Indirect Questions
- Emphasis and Inversion

## 5. Advanced Proficiency (C1)
**5.1 Chapter summary:** Near-native fluency with sophisticated grammar, idiomatic expressions, and the ability to express subtle meanings.

**2. Sub sections:**
- Future Perfect and Future Continuous
- Advanced Conditionals
- Subjunctive Mood
- Cleft Sentences
- Advanced Participle Clauses
- Nominalization
- Discourse Markers
- Collocation and Fixed Expressions
- Register and Formality

## 6. Mastery Level (C2)
**6.1 Chapter summary:** Native-like command of English with precision in all contexts, including academic, professional, and literary language.

**2. Sub sections:**
- Nuances in Tense and Aspect
- Advanced Stylistic Devices
- Hedging and Diplomatic Language
- Complex Sentence Structures
- Academic and Technical Vocabulary
- Idiomatic Language and Metaphor
- Pragmatics and Implicature
- Register Variation and Code-Switching
