# GRE English Preparation Book

Book objective: provide concise yet comprehensive preparation material for the GRE (Graduate Record Examination), focusing on verbal reasoning, analytical writing, and advanced vocabulary required for graduate-level academic success.

This book is designed for students who already have upper-intermediate to advanced English proficiency (B2-C1) and need to:
- Master GRE-level vocabulary (high-frequency and domain-specific words)
- Develop text completion and sentence equivalence strategies
- Improve reading comprehension for complex academic passages
- Enhance analytical writing skills for Issue and Argument essays

The chapters are structured around GRE test components and skill levels described in 00_chapters.md.
