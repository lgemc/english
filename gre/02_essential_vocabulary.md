## Roots, Prefixes, and Suffixes for Vocabulary Building

**Common Roots:**
- bene (good): benefit, benevolent, benefactor
- mal/male (bad): malevolent, malicious, malfunction
- cred (believe): credible, incredible, credit
- dict (speak): dictate, predict, contradict
- fid (faith): fidelity, confident, perfidy
- path (feeling): sympathy, empathy, apathy
- phil (love): philosophy, philanthropist
- poli/polis (city): politics, metropolis
- scrib/script (write): describe, manuscript
- voc/vok (call): vocal, evoke, provoke

**Common Prefixes:**
- a-/an- (without): amoral, anarchy
- anti- (against): antisocial, antidote
- auto- (self): autobiography, autonomous
- contra- (against): contradict, contrary
- de- (down, reverse): descend, debunk
- hyper- (excessive): hyperactive, hyperbole
- hypo- (under): hypothermia, hypothesis
- in-/im- (not): incomplete, impossible
- inter- (between): interact, intervene
- mis- (wrong): misunderstand, misjudge
- pre- (before): predict, preclude
- re- (again, back): return, reconcile
- sub- (under): subordinate, submarine
- trans- (across): transport, transcend

**Common Suffixes:**
- -able/-ible (capable of): readable, credible
- -acy/-cy (state/quality): democracy, accuracy
- -ity/-ty (state/quality): ability, certainty
- -ous/-ious (characterized by): curious, precious
- -tion/-sion (act/state): creation, tension
